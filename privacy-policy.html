Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2026 · Last Updated: March 24, 2026

Overview

Ad Blocker Sentinel ("the App") is an iOS ad blocker that blocks advertisements and trackers at the system level. Your privacy is fundamental to our mission — we built this app to protect it.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. Specifically:

  • No personal identifiers (name, email, phone number, Apple ID)
  • No device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV, device fingerprints)
  • No usage analytics or behavioral data
  • No browsing history or URL logs
  • No location data
  • No crash reports sent to external services
  • No third-party SDKs, trackers, or analytics frameworks

How the App Works

All ad and tracker blocking happens entirely on your device:

  • Bloom Filter: A compact data structure stored locally on your device checks URLs against known ad/tracker domains. No URLs leave your device during this check.
  • Private Information Retrieval (PIR): When a URL needs further verification, the query is sent through Apple's Oblivious HTTP (OHTTP) relay. This relay strips your IP address before the query reaches our server, ensuring we cannot identify who made the request.
  • Safari Content Blocker: Cosmetic ad-hiding rules run entirely within Safari on your device. Apple's Content Blocker API does not allow extensions to access browsing data.

Local Storage

The App stores preferences and settings locally on your device using iOS App Groups:

  • Filter enable/disable state
  • Whitelist (sites you've chosen to exclude from blocking)
  • Filter list selection preferences

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the App collects no personal data from any user, it is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

European Users (GDPR)

Since the App does not collect, process, or store any personal data as defined by the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), there is no personal data to access, rectify, delete, or port. The App is compliant with GDPR by design.

California Users (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), you have the right to know what personal information is collected about you. The App collects no personal information. We do not sell personal information, as we have none to sell. The App is compliant with CCPA.

Data Retention

No personal data is stored on our servers. Local preferences stored on your device are retained until you delete the App, at which point all local data is removed by iOS.
