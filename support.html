Quick Help

How do I enable ad blocking?
Open the app and follow the onboarding steps to enable the URL filter. You'll need to allow the network extension in Settings > General > VPN & Device Management.

Does this work with my VPN?
Yes. Ad Blocker Sentinel uses Apple's NEURLFilter API, which works alongside active VPN connections without occupying the VPN slot.

A site isn't loading correctly?
You can whitelist specific sites in the app's settings. If the issue persists, please report it so we can investigate.
